BANANA SHAKE



Banana milkshake is another amazing beverage for kids and adults alike. Along with a delicious taste this recipe of milkshake provides great amount of health benefits. A quick and easy milkshake that does wonders for your health and body. Crushed ice on top along with some cream and vanilla essence adds that extra thickness to this banana shake!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Banana, chopped
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Cream (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup Ice (crushed)

procedure

  • Put all ingredients in a blender jar and blend smooth.
  • To make it thicker you can also add some milk powder.
  • Pour the crushed ice on top.4.Serve chilled.
