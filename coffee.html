COLD COFFEE



Cold coffee is a creamy and popular cold beverage, that has mainly three ingredients â€“ instant coffee, milk, and sugar. Also called cold coffee shake or cold coffee milkshake, it is a perfect drink to sip on during hot days of summers. The best part of this cafe-style cold coffee is the frothy layer on top, which makes it taste even more appealing. This recipe will give you the exact same coffee, that you might have enjoyed in different cafes or restaurants. You just need a few ingredients and it will get ready in under 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • coffee powder
  • Milk
  • sugar
  • milk powder
  • ice cbes

procedure

  • Garnish two tall glasses by drizzling some chocolate syrup on the sides. Freeze the glasses for 10 minutes
  • Add 4 teaspoon instant coffee powder, 2 tablespoon sugar and 2 tablespoon warm milk in a small mixing bowl.
  • Mix until sugar is dissolved.
  • Add 2 cups chilled milk, 1 tablespoon milk powder, 3-4 ice cubes, and the coffee mixture to the medium jar of a blender and blend for a minute.
  • Pour the coffee into the prepared glasses. Add the froth on top using a spoon. Sprinkle some coffee powder on top. Serve chilled.
